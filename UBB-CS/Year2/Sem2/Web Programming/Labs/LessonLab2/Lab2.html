Origin

The heavy, dark, and full-bodied rums are the oldest type and have strong molasses flavour.

They are primarily produced in Jamaica, Barbados, and Demerara in Guyana.

Such rums are usually produced from molasses enriched with the skimmings, or dunder, remaining in the boilers used for sugar production.
